Is this for me?
If you have been cycling through protocols that do not hold, if your labs read "normal" but you feel off, or if you spent months going backwards on someone else's plan, the call is for you. If you are pregnant, in active psychiatric crisis, or in active cancer treatment, the right next step is your physician, not a coach.
Is Farren a doctor?
No. I am a certified functional health coach (NASM, AFAA Level 1). I do not diagnose, treat, or cure disease. I read your data, build the protocol around your biology, and work alongside your physician where one is in play.
